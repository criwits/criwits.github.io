Light in your Heart by Hans Wan

Waltzing the waves, shining my star,

Dark in the nights, light in your heart,

Carting the dreams, guiding us upward.

海波微微荡漾，璀璨一点星光，

午夜黑暗无常，心中自有光芒，

载着那份希望，引我们向前方。
